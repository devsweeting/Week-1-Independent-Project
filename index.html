A Short Bio:

Devin was born in the green wonder of the Pacific NW Portland-freakin-Oregon. He was raised in the heart of St. Johns. He grew up thinking he wanted to be Spider-man, but because radioactive power-giving spiders don't exist yet he's settled for a normal existence as a student, a son, a brother, a friend, and a caring cat-daddy.

He went to Benson Polytechnic HIghschool and studied automechanics and communications. After graduation he pursued higher education at Portland State University

The history of the penny of Great Britain and the United Kingdom from 1714 to 1901, the period in which the House of Hanover reigned, saw its transformation from a small silver coin to a larger bronze piece. All bear the portrait of the monarch on the obverse; copper and bronze pennies have a depiction of Britannia on the reverse.
